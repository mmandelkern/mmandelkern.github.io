Matthew Mandelkern

about
I am associate professor of philosophy at NYU, working on language, logic, etc.

I am DUS at NYU (FAQs); associate editor at Linguistics and Philosophy; area editor at Ergo; and co-organizer of New York Philosophy of Language Workshop.
